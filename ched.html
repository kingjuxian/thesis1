CHED E-Module

Module 1: Introduction

This module is all about introducing the fundamental concepts of teaching and learning with technology. We'll explore how technology can be effectively integrated into educational settings to enhance the teaching and learning experience.

Module 2: Policies and Issues

This module is all about examining the implications of ICT policies on technology and learning. Through this exploration, we'll unravel the complexities of regulatory frameworks and their impact on fostering effective technology integration in education.

Module 3: Teaching Tools

This module is all about exploring the intersection of non-digital and digital skills and tools in delivering technology-enhanced lessons. By striking a balance between traditional and modern teaching methods, educators can create dynamic learning experiences.

Module 4: Teaching Tools 2

This module is all about further delving into the integration of non-digital and digital skills and tools in delivering technology-enhanced lessons. Building upon the concepts introduced in Part 1, we'll continue to explore practical strategies for teaching and learning.

Module 5: Roles of Technology

This module is all about understanding the various roles technology plays in teaching and learning. By examining these roles, we'll uncover insights into how technology can be leveraged to enhance the educational experience for both teachers and students.
