TESDA E-Module

Module 1: Computer Hardware S..

This module is all about providing comprehensive training in computer hardware servicing. Participants will gain practical knowledge and skills essential for diagnosing, repairing, and maintaining computer hardware components, ensuring optimal functionality.

Module 2: Computer System S..

This module is all about equipping participants with the necessary expertise in computer system servicing. Through hands-on training and theoretical learning, individuals will learn to troubleshoot, repair, and optimize computer systems to meet industry standards and user requirements.

Activity: Computer System

This activity is all about reinforcing the concepts learned in the computer system servicing module. Participants will engage in practical tasks and scenarios to apply their knowledge and skills, consolidating their understanding of computer system servicing principles.

Assessment: Computer System

After completing this module, students will be able to identify and define the key functional components of output devices.

Module 3: Training Regulations

This assessment is all about evaluating participants' proficiency in computer system servicing. Through a series of practical tasks and theoretical assessments, participants will demonstrate their ability to diagnose, repair, and maintain computer systems according to industry standards.
